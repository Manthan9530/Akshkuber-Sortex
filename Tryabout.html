About Us

Since our inception in 2015, Akshkuber Sortex, based in Banera, Rajasthan, has been dedicated to enriching homes with superior food products, grounded in a commitment to quality and purity. From our modest beginnings, we've focused on meticulous processing and selection, ensuring that every product meets the highest standards. Through our renowned brands, Shantipurn, Charbhuja, and BFM, we consistently offer an exceptional range of Atta, Besan, Suji, Dalia, and Maida, which have become staples in countless kitchens.

Our strategic development of a powerful and far-reaching retail network has been instrumental in our journey. This extensive distribution capability has not only cemented our position as leaders in the packaged flour market within Rajasthan but has also driven our significant expansion across state lines. Today, Akshkuber Sortex is recognized as a trusted household name, with our products gracing pantry shelves across Rajasthan, Gujarat, and numerous other states. This widespread acceptance is a clear reflection of our innovative approach, unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction, and the consistent quality that defines the Akshkuber Sortex legacy. We are proud to be a part of daily meals, contributing to the health and well-being of families throughout the region and beyond.
